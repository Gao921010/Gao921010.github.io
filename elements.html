Our Story

A Journey of Passion, Dedication, and Canine Excellence

We are a collective of devoted dog lovers, united by our shared journey with the esteemed Sjouke Kooistra. Through his expert training programs, we have grown, learned, and developed a deep bond not only with our dogs but also with each other. Our journey has been one of sweat, hard work, joy, and at times, blood and tears. Together, we have faced challenges and celebrated triumphs, all while nurturing our dogs to become exceptional partners in their chosen fields. Our team is more than just a group of trainers; we are a family brought together by our love for dogs and our commitment to excellence. Under Sjoukeâ€™s mentorship, we have refined our skills, learning to train and handle dogs with the utmost care and professionalism. We laugh together, we work together, and we grow together, always striving to create the best possible outcomes for our dogs and ourselves.

Creating Future Canine Heroes

One of our greatest joys is seeing our dogs fulfill their potential in various professional roles. Whether they are serving as police dogs, detection dogs, or providing security, our dogs are living their dream jobs and making meaningful contributions to the world. Watching them work, knowing that they are helping to make our communities safer and more secure, fills us with immense pride and satisfaction.

A Commitment to Health and Capability

We are dedicated to the health, well-being, and capability of our dogs. Through continuous learning and collaboration, we strive to enhance their physical and mental attributes, ensuring they are not only fit for duty but excel in every aspect of their lives. Our goal is to show the world just how remarkable our furry friends can be, pushing the boundaries of what is possible and showcasing their incredible potential. We aim to bring a new perspective to those who may have little knowledge about the incredible capabilities of dogs. Our mission is to educate and inspire, demonstrating that dogs are not just pets but can be highly skilled and invaluable partners in various professional fields. We believe in the power of dogs to transform lives, contribute to society, and create lasting bonds with humans.
