Sjouke's Story

Early Beginnings in a Loving Family

Sjouke Kooistra's story begins in a warm, loving family, deeply connected with nature and animals. Growing up, he was surrounded by various animals, but it was his father’s Dobermans that sparked his lifelong passion for dogs. Under his father’s guidance, Sjouke learned the basics of dog sports and the essentials of responsible animal care. His mother’s nurturing influence taught him the importance of compassion and patience in dealing with animals. By the age of 13, Sjouke had already earned his first official dog training diploma, the “gehoorzaamheids diploma"(obedience diploma), marking the start of his journey into the world of professional dog training.

A Journey of Dreams and Achievements

At 27, after years of rigorous training and hard work, Sjouke's childhood dream came to fruition when he was recruited by the Royal Dutch Air Force as a military K9 handler. This was no small feat; it was the culmination of years spent honing his skills under the mentorship of his father, who was not only a dog trainer but also a boxing coach. Sjouke himself became a recognized Dutch boxing champion and earned his certification as a professional boxing trainer, showcasing his dedication to excellence in both disciplines.


During his tenure with the Royal Dutch Air Force, Sjouke's skills were put to the test in numerous high-pressure K9 training programs. His dedication and prowess led to multiple awards, certifications, and diplomas. One of the highlights of his career was his deployment to Afghanistan, where he and his K9 partner played critical roles in military operations, protecting airbases, and saving lives. In 2007, his outstanding performance and contributions to training AMK9 contract working dogs at Kandahar Airfield earned him a certificate of appreciation from American K9 Detection Services Inc., a testament to his exceptional capabilities and commitment.

Transition to Professional KNPV and Service Dog Training

After an illustrious career in the Air Force, Sjouke transitioned into the civilian sector, channeling his extensive experience into professional KNPV (Koninklijke Nederlandse Politiehond Vereniging) and service dog training. Initially, he was deeply involved in training police dogs for the Royal Dutch Police Force. However, his passion for dog training led him to establish his own service dog security business. This venture allowed him to expand his training programs beyond police work, encompassing a wide range of service dog roles, including surveillance, protection, and detection.
In his quest for excellence, Sjouke developed a unique training system tailored for law enforcement, which he transformed into comprehensive training programs. Concurrently, he launched a breeding program focused on enhancing the balance, performance, and health of working dogs. His innovative approaches and dedication quickly earned him a stellar reputation, not only in the Netherlands but also on the international stage. His work is recognized for its impact on improving the quality and effectiveness of service dogs worldwide.

Commitment to Excellence and Innovation

In recent years, Sjouke's focus has shifted towards more specialized training, particularly in preparing green dogs and trained dogs for deployment with special forces in combat scenarios. His hands-on experience in real-world combat conditions has enabled him to refine his training techniques, ensuring that these dogs are prepared for the toughest challenges. Sjouke's mission goes beyond just training; he is committed to advancing the genetic pool of working dogs and passing on his extensive knowledge to future generations in the service dog community.
Sjouke's legacy is not just one of a skilled trainer and handler, but also of a compassionate individual who has made significant contributions to the welfare of dogs and their handlers. He has rehabilitated numerous dogs with behavioral issues, giving them a second chance to serve in meaningful roles rather than facing euthanasia. His work has also helped many KNPV and IGP enthusiasts improve their training methods, leading to better outcomes for both dogs and handlers.

A Life Dedicated to Dogs

Sjouke Kooistra's life is a testament to his unwavering dedication to dogs and their well-being. He values the simple, yet profound bond between dogs and humans, a relationship built on loyalty, trust, and mutual respect. Sjouke believes that a well-trained service dog forms the foundation of a successful partnership, and that a skilled trainer can unlock a dog's true potential, transforming it from good to great. His commitment to enhancing the world of service dogs continues to drive him, as he tirelessly works to leave a lasting legacy in the field of dog training and breeding.
