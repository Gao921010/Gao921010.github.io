Our Story

Military K-9 Specialist:Dutch Air Force

For six years, Sjouke Kooistra served as a distinguished K-9 specialist in the Dutch Air Force, training dogs for real combat scenarios. During his tenure, he and his canine partners participated in numerous missions, including a significant deployment to Afghanistan. There, his expertly trained dogs performed critical roles in detecting explosives, conducting search and rescue operations, and providing protection, directly contributing to mission success and saving countless lives. Kooistra's commitment to excellence earned him numerous certifications, cementing his reputation as an extraordinary military dog professional.

Royal Dutch Police Dog Trainer(KNPV)

Following his military service, Sjouke Kooistra channeled his passion into training police dogs, becoming a key figure in the Royal Dutch Police Dog (KNPV) program. He specialized in preparing dogs for a variety of roles, including protection, search, and detection tasks. Under his guidance, countless high-quality police dogs were produced for both police and military use. His expertise in this field garnered him multiple prestigious certifications, such as the KNPV Instructor Certification, KNPV Decoy Certification, and KNPV Certification for Police Dog Handler, reinforcing his status as a leading police dog trainer.

Advanced Training and Breeding Programs

In recent years, Sjouke Kooistra expanded his focus to not only training dogs for law enforcement and security purposes but also offering specialized programs for handling working dogs and rehabilitating those with dangerous behaviors. He initiated the Frisank9 bloodline, a breeding program that emphasizes health, genetic improvement, and balanced temperament. This program aims to produce dogs that excel in various roles while maintaining robust health and stability, showcasing Kooistra's ongoing dedication to advancing the field of professional dog training and breeding.
