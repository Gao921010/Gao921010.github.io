    Expert in Service Dog Training for Law Enforcement

    Sjouke Kooistra is renowned for his exceptional skill in training service dogs tailored for law enforcement agencies. With a rich background in military K9 operations and extensive experience in high-stakes environments, Sjouke has honed his ability to train dogs for diverse roles, including surveillance, detection, and protection. His training methods emphasize reliability, precision, and adaptability, ensuring that each dog meets the rigorous standards required by police forces and special units. Whether it’s preparing dogs for patrol duties, search and rescue missions, or specialized detection tasks, Sjouke’s approach ensures that every service dog is a dependable and invaluable asset to law enforcement.

    Certified Decoy and Trainer of Trainers

    As a certified decoy, Sjouke Kooistra brings a unique blend of expertise and practical experience to the realm of dog training. He is not only adept at handling service dogs but also excels in training other trainers and decoys, imparting his knowledge and techniques to the next generation of professionals. His comprehensive training programs are designed to develop both the skills and the confidence required to manage high-pressure scenarios, ensuring that trainers and decoys are fully prepared for real-world challenges. Sjouke’s commitment to excellence extends beyond his own work, as he strives to elevate the standards of dog training within the community, fostering a culture of professionalism and continuous learning.

    Specialist in Selective Breeding: The Frisian K9 Bloodline

    Sjouke Kooistra is a dedicated specialist in the selective breeding of high-quality service dogs, with a particular focus on the prestigious Frisian K9 bloodline. His breeding program is rooted in a deep understanding of genetics, health, and performance, aimed at producing dogs that excel in both physical capabilities and temperament. The Frisian K9 bloodline is recognized for its superior traits, including intelligence, drive, and stability, making it ideal for demanding roles in law enforcement and military operations. Sjouke’s meticulous selection process ensures that each dog bred under his program not only meets but exceeds the highest standards of working dog excellence, contributing to the development of top-tier service dogs worldwide.

    Expert Services to meet your k-9 needs

    Sjouke Kooistra offers a comprehensive range of K-9 services, leveraging his extensive experience in military and police dog training to provide unparalleled expertise in the field.

      Sjouke Kooistra specializes in selecting and training high-potential dogs, preparing them for advanced field training and use in law enforcement. His green dogs are carefully chosen for their aptitude and readiness to excel in various law enforcement roles.

      Kooistra offers comprehensive training programs for dog handling officers, helpers, and trainers, equipping them with the skills needed for effective K-9 teamwork. His programs emphasize practical, real-world applications and high standards of training.

    • Vendor Services
