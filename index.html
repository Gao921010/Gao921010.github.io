Meet Sjouke Kooistra:
Expert Trainer and Breeder of Elite K9s

Sjouke Kooistra, a former Dutch K9 officer, brings a wealth of expertise and dedication to the world of professional dog training and breeding. With a storied career spanning law enforcement, military, and security sectors, Sjouke has become a recognized authority in training and handling dogs for some of the most demanding roles.

Military K-9 Specialist:Dutch Air Force

For six years, Sjouke Kooistra served as a distinguished K-9 specialist in the Dutch Air Force, training dogs for real combat scenarios. During his tenure, he and his canine partners participated in numerous missions, including a significant deployment to Afghanistan. There, his expertly trained dogs performed critical roles in detecting explosives, conducting search and rescue operations, and providing protection, directly contributing to mission success and saving countless lives. Kooistra's commitment to excellence earned him numerous certifications, cementing his reputation as an extraordinary military dog professional.

Royal Dutch Police Dog Trainer(KNPV)

Following his military service, Sjouke Kooistra channeled his passion into training police dogs, becoming a key figure in the Royal Dutch Police Dog (KNPV) program. He specialized in preparing dogs for a variety of roles, including protection, search, and detection tasks. Under his guidance, countless high-quality police dogs were produced for both police and military use. His expertise in this field garnered him multiple prestigious certifications, such as the KNPV Instructor Certification, KNPV Decoy Certification, and KNPV Certification for Police Dog Handler, reinforcing his status as a leading police dog trainer.

Advanced Training and Breeding Programs

In recent years, Sjouke Kooistra expanded his focus to not only training dogs for law enforcement and security purposes but also offering specialized programs for handling working dogs and rehabilitating those with dangerous behaviors. He initiated the Frisank9 bloodline, a breeding program that emphasizes health, genetic improvement, and balanced temperament. This program aims to produce dogs that excel in various roles while maintaining robust health and stability, showcasing Kooistra's ongoing dedication to advancing the field of professional dog training and breeding.

Expert Services to meet your k-9 needs

Sjouke Kooistra offers a comprehensive range of K-9 services, leveraging his extensive experience in military and police dog training to provide unparalleled expertise in the field.
From breeding top-tier working dogs and rehabilitating challenging behaviors to training law enforcement personnel and finding the ideal canine for any purpose, his services are tailored to meet the highest standards of quality and effectiveness.

  • Law Enforcement Dog Selling (Green Dog)

    Sjouke Kooistra specializes in selecting and training high-potential dogs, preparing them for advanced field training and use in law enforcement. His green dogs are carefully chosen for their aptitude and readiness to excel in various law enforcement roles.

  • Working Dog Breeding

    Kooistra meticulously breeds the Frisank9 bloodline, focusing on health, genetic excellence, and performance. Each dog in the program is registered with a BRN number, ensuring traceable lineage and quality.

  • Behavior Dog Rehabilitation

    Sjouke Kooistra rehabilitates aggressive civil and working dogs, transforming their behavior through expert intervention and training. His approach restores the dogs to a state of balance and functionality, allowing them to reintegrate effectively into their roles.

  • Law Enforcement Dog Training Program

    Kooistra offers comprehensive training programs for dog handling officers, helpers, and trainers, equipping them with the skills needed for effective K-9 teamwork. His programs emphasize practical, real-world applications and high standards of training.

  • KNPV Training Program

    Sjouke Kooistra provides specialized training for dog sports enthusiasts, guiding them towards obtaining KNPV or IGP certifications. His program focuses on developing dogs and handlers to achieve excellence in competitive dog sports.

  • Vendor Services

    Kooistra excels at finding and selecting ideal dogs tailored to specific needs, whether for personal, law enforcement, or security purposes. His extensive network and expertise ensure the perfect match for every requirement.
