Master Trainer Behind a Multi-Role K-9 Hero
1996

In 1996, Sjouke Kooistra demonstrated his exceptional skills as a K-9 specialist with the Dutch Air Force in Afghanistan. Amidst the intensity of active conflict, Kooistra trained Rex, a highly versatile and reliable canine whose capabilities extended beyond conventional roles. Under Kooistra's expert guidance, Rex excelled in tasks ranging from explosive detection to life-saving search and rescue operations. Kooistra’s training ensured that Rex could seamlessly transition between critical duties, safeguarding the lives of soldiers and enhancing operational effectiveness. This story underscores Kooistra’s mastery in developing K-9s that are not only indispensable in combat but also vital to the success of military missions.
